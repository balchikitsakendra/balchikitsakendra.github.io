About Us

BAL CHIKITSA KENDRA FOR PHYSICALLY HANDICAPPED is a national award winning NGO. This is a voluntary organisation registered under the Indian societies Registration Act of 1860.Established in 1997, it has been working towards creating education and livelihood opportunities for the disabled and disadvantaged communities.

The vision of the organization encompasses an inclusive society free of discrimination where people with disabilities, especially the physically impaired, enjoy equal opportunities. It has empowered disabled, physically impaired and underprivileged people through various initiatives in the education, livelihood, social enterprise, rehabilitation sport and cultural sectors, aiming to touch 100,000 lives with disability each year by 2020.

Objective

To promote the welfare and ensure training and education of special children of all ages

Vision

To promote the welfare and ensure training and education of special children of all ages. To undertake and encourage scientific and other research work related to mental retardation and to promote ameliorative and preventive measures. To help and advice parents and friends of special children and to foster mutual help between them. To gather and disseminate information on mental retardation and to collect all books and literature on the subject and to maintain a library open to the public. To develop better understanding of the problems of mental retardation by the public. To cooperate with the public, private and religious agencies and professional groups and organisations in the furtherance of these ends.

Mission

Undertake such activities that may be deemed incidental or conductive to the attainment of the above aims and objectives. Focus projects such as setting up Day care centres, Day schools, diagnostic clinics, Rehabilitation clubs, vocational training centres, holiday homes, canteens and similar institutions for the rehabilitation of the mentally retarded. To promote sports and culture activities among the disabled and take part in National and International meets/competitions.
